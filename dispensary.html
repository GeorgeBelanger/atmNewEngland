The Problem

Dispensaries Are
Locked Out of Card Processing

Federal banking restrictions prevent most dispensaries from accepting credit or debit cards. That's not changing anytime soon — but your customers still need to pay.

  • Card processors won't work with dispensaries Federal law classifies it as a controlled substance transaction. Banks pass.
  • Customers without cash leave empty-handed Every sale you lose to "I don't have cash" is money walking out the door.
  • An on-site ATM closes the gap instantly Customers withdraw cash right in your store and complete the purchase. No lost sales.
