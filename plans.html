Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?
Most installations are completed within a few days of signing. We handle delivery, setup, and testing — you just show us where you want it.
Is there a contract?
We work with service agreements that protect both parties. Terms depend on the plan and location — we'll walk you through everything before you sign anything.
What happens if the machine goes down?
Call us. We offer 7-day technical support and on-site service when needed. Most issues are resolved remotely within hours. For Full Placement and 50/50 plans, all repair costs are on us.
Can I switch plans later?
Yes — all plans are customizable and we're always open to renegotiating terms as your business grows. Many customers start on Full Placement and move to Owner Operated once they see the transaction volume.
Do you service dispensaries?
Yes. We specialize in ATM placement for Maine dispensaries that can't accept card payments due to federal banking restrictions. Our Full Placement plan is a popular option — zero cost to the dispensary, we handle everything. Learn more →

Not sure which plan is right for you?

Call us or fill out the form — we'll ask a few questions and point you in the right direction.
