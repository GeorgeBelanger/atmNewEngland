Our Story

Two Decades of Keeping New England Businesses Stocked With Cash

ATM New England was founded with one idea: local businesses deserved better service than they were getting from national ATM operators. Long response times, opaque fee structures, and machines that sat broken for days were the norm.

We built a company around the opposite model — fast response, transparent plans, and machines that actually stay up. Over 20 years we've grown our footprint across Maine and New England, staying focused on the same core customers: independent business owners who need a reliable partner, not a vendor.

Today we serve retail shops, restaurants, bars, hotels, event venues, and Maine dispensaries navigating the cash-only landscape. The business has changed a lot. The phone number hasn't.
